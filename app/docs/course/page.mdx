## Course structure

<Steps>
### Fourier Optics

The solution of Helmholtz equation, Raleygh-Sommerfeld diffraction integral

### Angular Spectrum Method

Plane wave decomposition, spatial response function.

### Diffractive Optical Elements

Amplitude and phase modulation, thin lens, diffractive layer, SLM.

### Optical Computers

Backpropagation, optimization methods (Adam, SGD), architectures D2NN, RNN, 4f-systems.
